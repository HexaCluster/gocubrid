## Contributor License Agreement (CLA) - required

Before we can accept your contribution, you must agree to the Contributor
License Agreement in [`CLA.md`](CLA.md). The CLA confirms that:

- you have the right to submit the contribution, and
- you grant HexaCluster Corp. the copyright and patent licenses described
  in the CLA over your contribution.

This protects both you and the project, and it keeps the ownership of the
project clear. You only need to agree to the CLA once; it covers all of
your future contributions to this project.

### How to agree

By submitting a pull request to this repository, you certify that you have
read the CLA in `CLA.md` and that you agree to its terms for the
contribution contained in that pull request.

If your contribution is made on behalf of an employer or another entity,
you confirm that you are authorized to agree to the CLA on its behalf.

## Submitting changes

1. Fork the repository and create a branch for your change.
2. Make your change with clear, focused commits.
3. Open a pull request describing what the change does and why.
4. Be available to respond to review feedback.
